OUR ROOMS

The room rate is all-inclusive and includes a multi-course gourmet breakfast for two, cooked to order by our resident chef. Breakfast is routinely served in our rustic breakfast room, where in the colder months we light a log fire in the walk-in fireplace, or in the conservatory. In the warmer months, weather permitting, you may prefer to enjoy breakfast al fresco on your own private deck or on our veranda overlooking the pool and grounds. And of course, you are always welcome to order breakfast in bed in the privacy of your suite.

RATES

Our rates are all-inclusive and include a multi-course, gourmet breakfast cooked-to-order by our resident chef. Depending on the season, you can enjoy breakfast in the privacy of your suite, in our beautiful fireside dining room or even dine al fresco on a verandah overlooking the pool and grounds.

Rates vary by season and by day of the week and all rates are subject to change. Being somewhat out of our own control, rates listed on 3rd party websites may not be accurate. To confirm the prevailing rate for your chosen dates click here. We occasionally offer last minute special rates for Sunday and midweek stays, especially during the winter months.

TAXES

Lodging taxes vary by county and by state. Here in Bucks County, PA we add sales tax of 6% and an area low 2.79% lodging tax. Be sure to include taxes when making rate comparisons. Tax is not applicable to in-suite spa services.

About our Rooms, Suites & Rates

The Inn at Bowman's Hill has 4 superior rooms and 4 suites including the all-new Penthouse and General Washington Suites. Considered by many to be the most romantic bed & breakfast in Bucks County, this New Hope Bed & Breakfast Inn is the perfect weekend getaway — and just 90 minutes from New York City.

All of our rooms and suites are beautifully appointed. King-size featherbeds, fireplaces, state-of-the-art bathrooms, heated 2-person whirlpool tubs, separate showers, heated towel racks and microfiber robes are the norm. In addition, five of the rooms and suites include a private veranda, all but one with panoramic views of the manicured grounds. Three of the suites feature a steam shower. The flagship Penthouse Suite has a corner tub with a starlight ceiling incorporating some 300 fiber optic stars and even a shooting star!

Traveling with friends or adult family? Two of our suites can accommodate up to four persons. Take a look at the cozy Tower Suite Garden View room for example. This optional 2nd bedroom can be reserved when booking the Regal Tower Suite and offers complete privacy with shared common space.

To experience the ultimate in relaxation and bonding, consider booking the optional simultaneous couple's massage in the privacy of your room or suite.

A detailed list of the amenities specific to each room can be viewed by clicking the "See Complete Room / Suite Details" link that can be found on each of the individual room / suite pages.
