INFORMATION FOR PRISONS

Service Overview

Buzzow Secure Server™ is a messaging platform that allows inmates to exchange voice and text messages with approved contacts that are already authorized by their correctional facility.

Buzzow™ does not forward inmate phone calls to unauthorized persons nor does it permit communication with phone numbers outside the inmate's contacts list. Click here for an overview of the service.

Monitoring by Correctional Facilities

An inmate's access to Buzzow Secure Server™ is limited to voice calls which are monitored by correctional facilities in the same way as any existing phone call from the inmate to an approved contact.

Buzzow™ does not enable or allow the inmate to communicate in any way which cannot be recorded by the correctional facility.

Benefits of Buzzow™ for Correctional Facilities

Providing a reliable service for communication between inmates and their outside supporters is crucial to the rehabilitation objectives put forth by all institutions.

Buzzow™ has found that inmates who use our service find it easier to maintain a healthy level of communication with a significant other, child, friend, attorney, or mentor through the use of our messaging services. Reducing stress within the inmate population has been found to improve health and mortality rates.

My Buzzow™

My Buzzow™ allows messaging with a single outside supporter. When an outside supporter signs up for My Buzzow™ they are issued a local number in the same area code as their inmate's correctional facility.

An introductory package is then mailed to the inmate with instructions indicating that they must add this local number to their inmate contact list. Once the number has been added and approved by their correctional facility, the inmate may begin utilizing Buzzow™ to send and receive messages with the outside supporter who initiated the account.
