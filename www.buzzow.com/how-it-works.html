HOW IT WORKS

Sending a Voice Message to Your Inmate

Sending a voice message to your inmate is so easy! Just dial your personal Buzzow™ phone number and press "3" at the main menu. The system will prompt you to start recording your message.

When you've completed your recording press "#". You'll then have options to press "1" to create the message, press "2" to re-record your message, or press "3" to cancel the recording.

Sending a Text Message to Your Inmate

Sending a text message is even easier! Just send a text message to your personal Buzzow™ phone number. That's it!!

Buzzow Secure Server™ will translate your text message to an audible voice message for your inmate to listen to the next time he checks his messages.

How Your Inmate Receives Messages

Your inmate will dial your personal Buzzow™ phone number and will be prompted to say his or her 4-digit PIN number. Once the inmate has identified themself, they are able to interact with our revolutionary software using voice commands.

At the main menu, the system will notify your inmate of the number of messages they have received. To listen to messages, the imate will press "1" from the main menu.

How Your Inmate Sends Messages

Your inmate can send you a text message by pressing "2" from the main menu. They will then record their message which will then be translated from voice to text by Buzzow Secure Server™ and delivered to your mobile phone.

Your inmate can record a voice message by pressing "3" from the main menu. Once recorded, the message will be available for you to listen to the next time you check your messages.
