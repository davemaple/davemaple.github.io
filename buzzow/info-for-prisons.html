Information for Prisons
Interlinx is a secure platform for inmates and their family members and friends to send and receive important, and sometimes, urgent messages. The overall functionality of our service is really no different than an inmates current ability to send and receive text or email messages through their loved ones and friends whom they speak to over the phone daily; and in all honesty, this actually takes place everyday in jails and prisons across the country. The significant difference between any messages passed through the abilities of a family member or friend, versus messages passed through our service, is the multiple levels of security that Interlinx has in place to ensure a safe and professional channel for message communication.​

Inmates and their outside supporters who communicate in this manner through Interlinx as their alternative contact point, will not only reduce stress within the institutions among inmates, but also for the family and friends of inmates as well. Having a reliable alternative vehicle for communication between inmates and their outside support, is crucial to the rehabilitation objectives put forth by all institutions. Interlinx provides that inmates that use our service:

1) will spend less time fighting over the phone with other inmates because they are hanging up and calling back because they can't get through to the person they are trying to reach; thus, causing stress for inmates that are waiting to use the phones,

2) inmates will argue less with outside supporters when they are unable to reach them by phone all day long, but have something important to say to them, because now the inmate can leave a voice message or send a text message and know the message will get through, and vice versa,

3) our serious and professional use policy promotes a better communication etiquette by prohibiting inmates from using foul and inappropriate language in their messages, and the list goes on as to how Interlinx is a useful and secure tool, and just how much it really does reduce stress and tension levels for everyone involved.
